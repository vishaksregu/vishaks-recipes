Overnight Peanut Butter Oats

Oats, oats, oats. No doubt the arch-nemesis of millions of kids around the world. Or was it just me? Probably just me. Growing up, I hated Oats with a burning passion. It just did not have enough flavour

But 18 years, a global pandemic, several god awful romcoms, some awesome days at the gym, and a couple hikes later. Here I am, professing this overnight oats recipe

But it's not just any oats, it is Overnight Peanut Butter Oats

Ingredients

  • Rolled Oats - 1⁄2 Cup
  • Milk (preferably oat milk) - 11⁄2 Cup
  • Yoghurt - 1⁄3 Cup
  • Maple Syrup - to taste
  • Assorted nuts & dried fruits - to taste
  • Peanut Butter - 1 Teaspoon
  • Blue Better - optional

Instrutions

  1. Blend the Milk, yoghurt and peanut butter together for a few seconds
  2. Add the oats to the blended mixture with a pinch of salt
  3. Mix well. Add blueberries, and leave it covered in the fridge overnight
  4. Serve with maple syrup, fried nuts, fresh fruits and optionall dry fruits
